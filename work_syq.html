Eating Instrument 乐食器

Basic Info
Type: Interactive Installation
Tool: Processing / Arduino /Spacebrew / Openframeworks
Team member: 3
Work in charge: Software Programming / Hardware Hacking

Requirement & Purpose
The instrument detected the shape of the food sticked to the ‘chopstick fork’ by sensoring the distence between the ultrasonic sensor and the surface of the food. A motor was used to rotate the fork not only for eating easily but also for generating different signals.
A visual effect was developed according to the distance signal and to trigger different music and sound samples. The players may perform their one and only piece by eating defferent food.
